How to use it

  • Search existing threads before posting — someone may have already asked your question.
  • Post a specific question about a reading, an assignment, or something from lecture that didn't sit right with you.
  • Reply to a classmate's thread if you know the answer, or have a different take. This isn't just for the instructor to answer.
  • A GitHub account is free and takes a minute to set up if you don't already have one.
