Climate Change Governance: Science, Data & Models

ENGG562 / ENV462 · Syed Babar Ali School of Science & Engineering, LUMS

This interdisciplinary course equips students with the tools, models, and frameworks necessary to understand and influence climate, water, energy, and food policy through data-driven systems thinking. It blends technical modeling with governance insights into climate adaptation, sustainable agriculture, and infrastructure resilience. Students engage in scenario-based planning, explore socio-technical systems, and work with real-world case studies from the Indus Basin.
